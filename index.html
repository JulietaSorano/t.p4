About us

The rush has fostered a new way of enjoying the pleasures of life very similar to the American style that they have tried to impose on us in recent decades. At Travelers, we believe that slow travel means enjoying traveling, without rushing, opening your eyes and heart to everything that surrounds you. This culture focuses more on quality than quantity, advocates disconnecting from technology, slowing down and enjoying the moments: in short, improving your quality of life. Take an interest in the local, make local plans, go to local restaurants and taverns to try the typical dishes, run away from fast food or international food restaurants. Stay in rural houses, hostels run by local people or rental houses. Accumulate special moments and experiences, anecdotes and personal stories instead of postcard photos.
